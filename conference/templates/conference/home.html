About the Conference

Uganda Christian University through the department of computing and technology in conjunction with Uganda communication commission, MTN Uganda, Innovation village, Outbox and Google are calling for participants in this upcoming conference. The conference will take 4 days at Nkoyoyo hall where different researcher will present their trending works. It is the only conference of its kind where attendees can obtain real-world knowledge and training that is free. The conference features sessions on the full scope of digital transformation technologies including Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Observability, DevOps, Performance Engineering, Digital Process, Cloud Native Applications, and the IT infrastructures that support them. And for the very first time this conference will be hosted at Uganda Christain University.
